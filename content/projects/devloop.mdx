## Problem

Devloop needed a public site that could explain the agency quickly in both English and Arabic. It also needed to be fast, easy to crawl, and practical enough for real inquiries instead of ending at a generic contact section.

## Solution

I built a localized Next.js site with RTL support, theme switching, careful loading around the animated parts of the UI, and a Resend-backed contact form.

## Architecture

The project uses Next.js 16 App Router, React 19, and Tailwind CSS 4. `next-intl` handles English and Arabic routing, `next-themes` handles color mode, Framer Motion powers the motion layer, and Resend sends contact email.
