## Problem

Dana Doors needed a site that could work as a real catalog, not just a few showroom pages. The important parts were language support, clear browsing, strong product imagery, and pages that could still make sense to search engines.

## Solution

I built an English and Arabic website with RTL layouts, organized product paths, Cloudinary-backed galleries, dark and light themes, and the usual discovery work around metadata, sitemap output, and structured data.

## Architecture

The site runs on Next.js 16 App Router, TypeScript, and Tailwind CSS. Radix UI and shadcn/ui handle the interface primitives, Cloudinary owns image delivery, and Upstash Redis supports lightweight analytics.
